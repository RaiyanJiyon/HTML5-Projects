Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United Stated?

The Taco was first introduced to the United States in 1905. Mexican migrants were coming in to work on railroads and other jobs and started to bring their delicious food with them. Tacos were essentially a street food at this time since they were highly portable and cheap. (Source: History Of The Taco )
